# A1 - Nature / Science Journal

The house style for dense quant posters. Colored masthead, italic abstract, two-column justified body, numbered Figure/Table panels with bold captions, and a shaded caveat box.

See `style.yaml` for the reusable prompt scaffold and full metadata.

## Example renders

- `examples/hurst.png` - the Hurst exponent
- `examples/stacking-carry.png` - oil + bonds synthetic TIPS
- `examples/tips-vs-synthetic.png` - TIPS vs synthetic pricing

(Drop the PNGs into `examples/`. The prompts that generated them are reconstructable from the scaffold in `style.yaml`.)
